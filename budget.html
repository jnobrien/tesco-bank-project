Savings Puzzle

Disclaimer: This budget game is a puzzle experience designed for entertainment purposes only and is not intended to simulate real-world financial scenarios.

Objectives

  1. The player will be able to accurately distinguish between needs and wants.
  2. The player will be able to create a budget that aligns with Tesco Bank's recommendation.

How to Play

  1. Click and drag items in the purchases area to the "Needs" or "Wants" box.
  2. Use the buttons in the "Savings" box to increase or decrease your savings amount.
  3. To solve the puzzle, meet your exact savings goal in exactly 10 paychecks.
